Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MyForexSignature Trading Academy (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you visit our website, enroll in our forex and crypto courses, or join our signal and mentorship services.

By accessing or using our website and services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Who We Are

MyForexSignature Trading Academy provides educational content, mentorship, and trading signals for forex and crypto markets. Our services are for educational purposes only and do not constitute financial or investment advice.

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to handling your data in a fair, transparent, and secure way.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to deliver and improve our trading education and signal services. The types of information we may collect include:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you interact with us, you may provide the following information:

•  Account information: Name, email address, phone number, password, country.

•  Contact details: Information you provide when you contact us via forms, email, WhatsApp, Telegram, or social media.

•  Billing details: Payment-related information (handled via secure third-party processors). We do not store full card details on our servers.

•  Learning and trading preferences: Experience level, markets you trade (forex/crypto/indices), time zone, and interests.

•  Signal group details: Your messaging handle or username (e.g. WhatsApp/Telegram) for adding you to private channels.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, some information is collected automatically:

•  Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, language settings.

•  Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, clicks, referring/exit pages, and general interaction with our content.

•  Cookies and similar technologies: Small text files stored on your device to remember your preferences, improve performance, and analyze traffic.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

•  IPayment providers (e.g., confirmation of successful payment or subscription).

•  Email marketing and CRM tools used to send newsletters and course updates.

•  Analytics providers that help us understand how visitors use our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

•  Service delivery: To create and manage your account, provide access to courses, mentorship, and signal groups.

•  Communication: To send you important updates about your account, courses, signals, and changes to our terms or policies.

•  Educational signals: To deliver trade ideas and market breakdowns via email, WhatsApp, Telegram, or other communication channels you opt into.

•  Customer support: To respond to your messages, questions, and feedback.

•  Improvement and analytics: To analyze how our website and services are used so we can improve content, user experience, and performance.

•  Marketing (with consent where required): To send newsletters, special offers, and information about new courses or services you may be interested in.

•  Security and compliance: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or technical issues, and to comply with legal obligations.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our website effectively and to understand how visitors interact with it.

We may use the following types of cookies:

•  Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security.

•  Performance and analytics cookies: Help us understand website traffic and usage patterns.

•  Preference cookies: Remember your settings and choices (such as language or login details).

•  Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads or track the performance of our campaigns (if applicable).

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling some cookies may affect how certain parts of our website function.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with trusted third parties only in the following situations:

We may share information with third-party vendors who help us deliver our services, such as:

We may disclose information if we believe in good faith that it is necessary to:

•  Comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

•  Protect the rights, property, or safety of MyForexSignature, our users, or others.

5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change where required by law.

6. Data Security and Retention

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

These measures may include:

•  Using HTTPS/SSL to secure data transmitted between your browser and our website.
