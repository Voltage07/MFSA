Do I need any trading experience to join MyForexSignature?

No. Our training is designed for complete beginners as well as active traders. We start with the basics of forex and crypto markets, then gradually move into technical analysis, risk management, and live market examples.

What markets do you cover in your training and signals?

We primarily focus on major forex pairs and high-liquidity crypto assets. Some plans also include indices and gold, depending on market conditions and the strategy being taught.

How do your forex and crypto signals work?

Our signals include entry price, stop-loss, and take-profit levels, along with brief market context. They are sent via Telegram or WhatsApp, depending on your chosen plan. We always advise using proper risk management with each trade idea.

Do you manage funds or trade on behalf of clients?

No. MyForexSignature is an education and signals brand. We do not offer account management or investment services. You retain full control over your trading accounts and trading decisions at all times.

What are the risks of trading forex and crypto?

Forex and crypto trading involve a high level of risk and can lead to the loss of your entire capital. Leverage can amplify both profits and losses. We strongly recommend using proper risk management and only trading money you can afford to lose.
